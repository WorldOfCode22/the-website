About Me!

Education

I have a Bachelors of Science in Biology from Winthrop University. From my biology classes I learned how to think logically and critically, communicate and work with fellow students, create and deliver presentations, and how to think through complicated systems and their interactions. Outside of biology I learned the basics of Python, Access and the basics of creating a web page.

During my last two semesters and after college I turned most of my free focus to web development. I have spent numerous hours on youtube and digging through the documentation of the various libraries, frameworks, and languages in order to learn them. I am always hungry to learn more about application development. I enjoy learning how I can do things better and teaching others.

From Biology to Web Development

During my time in college, I had not really been excited to learn 90% of the biology that was taught. It felt like the only skills I had learned in biology was how to follow protocols on a sheet. Even common things like Gram stains we only did once or twice. Between not learning skills, limited opportunities in my area of interest and changing interest I felt it was time for a change in my life.

During the peak of my frustration and aggravation at the situation, I came across a video that was comparing self-taught, code bootcamp, and college as methods of learning to program. That day (October 1, 2017) I went to one of the websites the video suggested, freecodecamp, and started my journey into web development.
